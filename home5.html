BLACK HOLE

A black hole is a region of spacetime where gravity is so strong no particles or even electromagnetic radiation such as light can escape from it.

ASTERIOD BELT

The asteroid belt is a torus-shaped region in the Solar System, located roughly between the orbits of the planets Jupiter and Mars.

MILKYWAY GALAXY

The Milky Way is the galaxy that includes our Solar System, with the name describing the galaxy's appearance from Earth.
